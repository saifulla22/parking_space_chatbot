PARKING FACILITY INFORMATION
=============================

General Information:
Our parking facility offers secure, covered parking in the city center.
We provide 24/7 security surveillance and modern access control systems.
The facility has 200 spaces across 4 floors.
Electric vehicle charging stations are available on Floors 1 and 2 (20 stations total).

Location:
Address: 123 Main Street, City Center
Nearest metro: Central Station (2-minute walk)
GPS Coordinates: 52.5200 N, 13.4050 E
Landmarks: Located next to City Hall, opposite the Central Bank.

Working Hours:
Monday - Friday: 06:00 - 23:00
Saturday: 07:00 - 22:00
Sunday: 08:00 - 20:00
24/7 access is available for monthly subscribers only.

Prices:
Hourly rate: $3.00 per hour
Daily rate: $20.00 per day (up to 24 hours)
Weekly rate: $100.00 per week
Monthly subscription: $250.00 per month
Electric vehicle surcharge: $1.00 per hour (includes charging)

Availability:
The facility typically has 200 total spaces. Availability varies by day.
Pre-booking is recommended for weekends and public holidays.

Reservation Process:
1. Provide your full name (first name and last name).
2. Provide your vehicle registration number (car number).
3. Specify your reservation start date and end date.
4. Your request is reviewed by our administrator.
5. You receive a confirmation once approved.
Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance.

Parking Rules:
- Maximum vehicle height: 2.1 meters.
- No overnight parking without a reservation.
- Disabled parking spaces are available on Floor 1.
- No storage of hazardous materials in vehicles.
- Speed limit inside the facility: 10 km/h.
- Parking in non-reserved areas is not permitted.
